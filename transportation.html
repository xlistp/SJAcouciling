Transportation



Taxi

A taxi from the airport to Shinhwa World will cost approx. $25 USD. It is recommended to use this option.

Bus Schedule

You may take the 151 bus from the airport and it stops at Shinhwa World. You can board this bus at Gate 4 of the Jeju International Airport.
